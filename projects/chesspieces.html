Chess pieces

Modifying, 3D printing and hand painting custom chess pieces

We will use these pieces for our Chess table project

Video coming soon...

Our first goal was to find some epic chess pieces for our chess table build, after many hours of crawling the internet we found some great ones at the free3d website (see "Original 3D printing files" for links).
We modify them to size them down to our table size, changed the kings crown a bit, and completely re-worked the knight piece.
We weren't a big fan of their pawn pieces so decided to use the rook design as our pawns and use the awesome rook we found previously on Thingiverse.
it is more castle like so the pieces are intuitive even for someone that has not seen them before.

Big shout out and many thanks to the users that up-loaded these 3D models. printable_models and seechless

Next came the massive task of printing them all
my home made 3D printer go pushed hard and did a perfect job of the monumental work ahead of it (120+ hours of printing)

We are currently in the process of hand painting them.
and will put together a video of the whole process (we will post it on our youTube channel and here when done)
